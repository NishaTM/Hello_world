Professional Background

Dr. Nisha Thorakkattu Madathil is currently serving as a Visiting Faculty at the College of Information Technology, United Arab Emirates University (UAEU), Al Ain. Before this, she worked as an Adjunct Faculty at the same university, where she contributed to teaching and research in the fields of computer science and information security.

Earlier, Dr. Nisha served as a Research Assistant at UAEU, where she was actively involved in projects related to secure data analytics and federated learning. Prior to joining UAEU, she worked as a Lecturer and IT Trainer at Syscoms College, Al Ain, and as a Lecturer at MES Engineering College, Kerala, India, where she taught various computer science and engineering courses.

She began her professional career as a Programmer at UST Global, Trivandrum, gaining practical experience in software development and system programming.
